Landscaping & Gardening Course

Course Overview

This course will provide participants with the skills needed to create and maintain beautiful outdoor spaces. It covers various topics, including plant selection, garden design, maintenance techniques, and sustainable practices.

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding soil types and plant needs
  • Garden planning and design
  • Plant identification and care
  • Tools and equipment usage
  • Eco-friendly landscaping practices

    • Requirements

    No prior experience is required, but a passion for gardening and nature is encouraged!

    Course Duration

    This course runs for six months, with weekly sessions to provide hands-on training.
