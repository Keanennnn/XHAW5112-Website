Home Cooking Skills Course

Course Overview

This course teaches essential cooking skills for everyday meals, focusing on ingredients, techniques, and kitchen safety. Perfect for those who want to improve their cooking at home.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic cooking techniques and methods
  • Ingredient selection and preparation
  • Meal planning and budgeting
  • Food safety and kitchen hygiene
  • Healthy and balanced meal creation

    • Requirements

    No prior cooking experience required, just an interest in learning home cooking essentials.

    Course Duration

    This course runs for six months with weekly hands-on sessions to enhance practical cooking skills.
