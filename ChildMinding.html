Advanced Child Minding Course

Course Overview

The Advanced Child Minding course is designed to equip participants with essential skills for professional child care, including early childhood development, safety protocols, and nurturing practices.

What You'll Learn

  • Principles of early childhood development
  • Health, hygiene, and safety for young children
  • Managing daily routines and activities
  • Communicating with children and parents effectively
  • Identifying and responding to special needs

    • Requirements

    No prior experience is required, but a passion for working with children and patience are encouraged.

    Course Duration

    This course spans six months with weekly sessions, providing hands-on training and guidance in child care.
