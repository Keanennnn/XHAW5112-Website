Garden Maintenance Course

Course Overview

This course focuses on the essential skills needed to maintain a healthy garden. It covers various aspects of garden upkeep, including plant care, pest management, and seasonal tasks.

What You'll Learn

  • Plant health and maintenance techniques
  • Pest and disease management
  • Seasonal garden care and preparation
  • Soil health and improvement methods
  • Watering techniques and irrigation management

    • Requirements

    No prior gardening experience is needed, just a passion for plants and the outdoors!

    Course Duration

    This course runs for six months with weekly practical sessions for hands-on learning.
