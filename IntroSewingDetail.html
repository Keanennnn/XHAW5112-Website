Intro to Sewing Course

Course Overview

This beginner's course provides essential sewing skills, from handling fabrics to understanding basic sewing techniques. Ideal for those interested in exploring the world of tailoring and design.

What You'll Learn

  • Introduction to sewing tools and equipment
  • Understanding different fabric types
  • Basic sewing techniques and stitches
  • Simple garment construction
  • Repair and alteration basics

    • Requirements

    No prior experience required. This course is designed for beginners with an interest in sewing and tailoring.

    Course Duration

    This course spans six months, with weekly hands-on sessions to develop fundamental sewing skills.
