Housekeeping & Hospitality Course

Course Overview

This course provides participants with skills in housekeeping and hospitality practices, ideal for those aiming to work in hotels, guest houses, or private homes. Training includes cleaning techniques, customer service skills, and efficient management practices.

What You'll Learn

  • Effective cleaning and sanitization techniques
  • Room preparation and maintenance
  • Laundry care and ironing
  • Customer service fundamentals
  • Time and resource management
  • Etiquette and professional behavior in hospitality settings

    • Requirements

    No prior experience is needed; all that's required is an interest in housekeeping and hospitality services.

    Course Duration

    This course is conducted over six months with regular weekly sessions that combine practical training and classroom learning.
