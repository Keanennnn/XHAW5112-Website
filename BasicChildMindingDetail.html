Basic Child Minding Course

Course Overview

This course provides foundational skills for safe and responsible child care. Participants will learn essential child-minding techniques, focusing on safety, health, and effective caregiving practices.

What You'll Learn

  • Child safety and injury prevention
  • Age-appropriate play and learning activities
  • Nutrition basics for young children
  • Handling behavioral challenges
  • Emergency procedures and first aid for kids

    • Requirements

    No previous experience required, but a love for working with children is recommended.

    Course Duration

    This is a six-month course, with sessions held weekly to provide hands-on experience and theoretical knowledge.
