About Us


Who We Are


Empowering the Nation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Our mission is to uplift communities through education and practical skill development.


Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills they need to succeed in their careers and improve their quality of life.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual has access to education and opportunities that enable them to thrive and contribute to their communities.
