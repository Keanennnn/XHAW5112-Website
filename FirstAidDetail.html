First Aid Essentials Course

Course Overview

This course teaches essential first aid skills for dealing with emergencies. It includes training on life-saving techniques, wound care, CPR, and more to help individuals respond effectively in critical situations.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic first aid principles
  • CPR and rescue breathing
  • Handling cuts, bruises, and burns
  • Managing choking incidents
  • Recognizing and responding to shock
  • Handling sprains, fractures, and other injuries

    • Requirements

    No prior medical knowledge is required, just a willingness to learn how to assist in emergencies.

    Course Duration

    This comprehensive course spans six months with weekly sessions covering hands-on practice and theoretical knowledge.
